Overview

I built a bunch of simple flash games throughout high school. These games were created using with Flash + ActionScript 2.0 but most of the time I would find tutorials on how to build similar games, and model my games of them. ActionScript 2.0 was my first exposure to any sort of programming although I don't remember it all that well. At the time, I knew enough to know what each block of code was doing and how to manipulate it, but not so much why it was doing that. Despite being relatively simple, these games did surprisingly quite well online, totaling just over 150,000 views to date. Because of that, and the fact that they were my first exposure to programming, I've included them on this website.
